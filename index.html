Values and Policies to Uphold

As exec, I would intend to uphold vital existing values and policies while also striving to continuously improve upon certain aspects within CSESoc.

Community

I will continue to ensure that the community within CSESoc remains one that is transparent, honest, collaborative and above all inclusive. This involves:

  • Maintaining a high level of engagement from students in CSESoc events, initiatives and across different platforms like Discord and Facebook.
  • Stronger and more meaningful connections between students, as well as with CSE staff and industry sponsors.
  • Further interaction and collaboration with students both internally between different portfolios and from different societies and universities.

Support

Any member of this society will continue to be supported in all their social, academic and professional needs. This is achieved by:

  • Creating higher quality events with a goal of an average of 90% satisfaction rate from all events through feedback forms.
  • Inclusion of a dedicated HR/Internal team to ensure the mental health and wellbeing of society members.
  • Provide an avenue for CSE students to voice for events and initiatives they would like CSESoc to run.
